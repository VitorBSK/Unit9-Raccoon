
# Code of Conduct — Unit09 Project

## 1. Purpose

The goal of the Unit09 community is to build a collaborative ecosystem around
a story‑driven on‑chain AI raccoon. We value respect, openness, and constructive
participation. This Code of Conduct sets expectations for all contributors,
maintainers, users, and community participants.

By engaging with the project — including GitHub discussions, pull requests,
issues, documentation updates, or chat channels — you agree to follow this
Code of Conduct.

---

## 2. Expected behavior

All community members are expected to:

- Treat others with respect, professionalism, and kindness.
- Assume good intentions, ask questions before making negative assumptions.
- Provide helpful and constructive feedback.
- Welcome new members and help them succeed.
- Use inclusive language and avoid stereotypes.
- Respect boundaries and maintain personal and professional safety.

---

## 3. Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include, but are not limited to:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination or hateful conduct based on gender, race, ethnicity,
  nationality, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected
  status.
- Sexualized or explicit content, jokes, or imagery.
- Threats, intimidation, doxxing, or sharing of private information.
- Sustained disruption of conversations, PR reviews, or meetings.
- Using project resources for bullying or targeted harm.
- Impersonating maintainers or falsely claiming authority.

---

## 4. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub repositories under the Unit09 organization or contributors
- Discord, Telegram, Twitter (X), forums, or mailing lists hosted by the
  project
- Online or offline events where community members represent Unit09
- Any interaction in which the project brand is associated

---

## 5. Reporting violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately.

Contact:
- Email: security@unit09.org
- GitHub security advisory: https://github.com/unit09/security

When reporting, please include:

- A description of what happened
- Date, context, links or screenshots if available
- Whether it is safe for us to contact you

Reports will be handled confidentially, and reviewed by the policy committee.

---

## 6. Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate action, including:

- Warning or temporary moderation
- Reverting or rejecting harmful contributions
- Removal from chat, repositories, or events
- Permanent ban in cases of severe violations

Maintainers must act fairly, prioritize safety, and document decisions.

---

## 7. No retaliation

Retaliation against someone who submitted a report is a violation. Good‑faith
reports are protected even if the investigation concludes no violation occurred.

---

## 8. Community values

Unit09 aims to foster an ecosystem centered around:

- Curiosity and exploration
- Open‑source access and learning
- Respectful debate and shared ownership
- Support for new developers entering Solana

---

## 9. Changes

Policy updates will be timestamped and committed to the repository. Contributors
will be notified via community channels.

---

## 10. Acceptance

By participating in Unit09, you confirm that you have read, understood,
and agree to follow this Code of Conduct.
